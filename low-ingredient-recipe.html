5 Ingredients or Less: Tomato Feta Pasta

Image: Tomato Feta Pasta from All Recipes at allrecipes.com/article/10-easy-dinners-with-5-ingredients-or-less/

The list of ingredients are:

  • 10 ounce package of cherry tomatoes
  • olive oil
  • one tablespoon of minced garlic
  • ~ 8oz block of feta cheese
  • one box of the pasta of your choice

I found this recipe on Tik Tok and immediately tried it because I love the ingredients and really enjoyed that it doesn't require dirtying a lot of dishes. A description of the recipe can be found HERE, but since it's a very simple recipe, the steps are as follows:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Wash and place a pack of cherry tomatoes in the baking dish
  3. Drizzle about 3 tablespoons of olive oil above the top of the tomatoes and add in a tablespoon of minced garlic and mix it so the oil coats all of the tomatoes
  4. Sprinkle salt and pepper over the tomatoes as desired
  5. Place the full block of feta in the middle of the dish
  6. Once the oven is preheated, place the baking dish in the oven and cook for 30-35 minutes.
  7. While the tomatoes and feta are in the oven, cook your pasta based on the insturctions on the box and drain the noodles when done
  8. Once the tomatoes and feta are out of the oven, stir up the mixure while it's hot so it breaks up and forms a thick sauce consistency
  9. Add the noodles to the mixture

This is one of my favorite meals to make when I am on a tight budget week during the school year and most of these ingredients are relatively inexpensive. Enjoy!
