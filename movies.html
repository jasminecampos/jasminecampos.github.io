Movies I've Watched Recently

Top Gun Maverick: 9.5/10

I am not sure if this is a hot take or not, but I enjoyed Top Gun Maverick more than the original Top Gun. It had a lot to do with the plot as well as the effects. In the original, I felt very lost throughout the movie and thought it didn't quite follow an organized timeline. I like all of the elements incorporated from the original into Maverick, like Great Balls of Fire, bringing back Iceman, etc. I also LOVE Miles Teller which definitely played into this rating.

Where the Crawdads Sing: 7/10

I gave this movie a 7 because this is one of those movies where I read the book before watching the movie. While I thought the producers did a great job of keeping things relatively consistent, I think that the movie just didn't completely do the book justice (as usual). This movie still has an amazing plot followed by a twist at the end. It's got everything, mystery, romance, action, and drama.

Sixteen Candles: 10/10

I had to include this movie because it is my all time favorite comfort movie. I recently watched it twice on the coach bus from Madison to Ohare Airport when I was traveling to San Juan Puerto Rico. It's super funny, has a pretty good romance arc between Sam and Jake and reminds me a lot of my dysfucntional families. I have probably seen this movie 20 times and will watch it a thousand more.

Don't Worry Darling: 6/10

This one is definitely going to be controversial because so many people love this movie. I genuinely think it's only because of the cast, because the plot, while interesting, was very predictable and slow. I'd give 3 points for the cast, 2 for the plot, and 1 for part of it being set in a 50s-esque time period, I thought that was fun with the cars and outfits.
